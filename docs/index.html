Emoji Faceoff

Mimic the emoji. Guess the face!

How to Play

  1. One player holds the phone and sees an emoji
  2. They mimic the expression (without speaking!)
  3. Other players try to guess which emoji it is
  4. Tap "Got it!" if someone guesses correctly, or "Skip" to pass
  5. See how many your group can get right!

Frequently Asked Questions

How many players do I need?

At least 2 - one to mimic and one to guess. More players = more fun!

Does it work offline?

Yes! Once loaded, the app works completely offline. No internet needed.

How do I change the language?

Tap the flag icon in the top-left corner of the start screen.

How do I enable dark mode?

Tap the moon/sun icon in the top-right corner of the start screen.

Do you collect any data?

Nope! Zero data collection. See our Privacy Policy.

The emojis look different on my device?

Emojis are rendered by your device's operating system, so they may look slightly different on iOS vs Android vs other platforms.
