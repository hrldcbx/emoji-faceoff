Privacy Policy

Emoji Faceoff

The Short Version

We don't collect any data. Period.

Emoji Faceoff is a simple party game that runs entirely on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no ads.

Information We Collect

None. Zero. Nada.

  • No personal information
  • No usage data
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cookies (except what your browser may store locally)
  • No third-party services

Data Storage

The app stores two preferences locally on your device using your browser's localStorage:

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Language preference

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by clearing your browser data or app data.

Third-Party Services

We don't use any. No analytics, no crash reporting, no ad networks, nothing.

Children's Privacy

Since we don't collect any data, there's nothing to worry about here. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (unlikely, since there's nothing to change), we'll update this page.

Contact

Questions? Reach out at support@example.com or visit our support page.
